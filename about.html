Writer & Reader of Feelings

Chada Montsho

I started Paradoxical Pages because I've always found comfort in books that understand the harder feelings the melancholy, the longing, the quiet moments of solitude that speak volumes.

As someone who writes poetry and studies psychology, I've learned that the feelings we're told to hide anxious attachment, avoidance, the ache of yearning are often the ones that connect us most deeply to each other and to art.

Why "Paradoxical Pages"?
"Sometimes it is diffult to truly express and understand an emotion one can feel so this liitle sanctuary helps know that you are not alone and writing truly is a writer's way of breathing"
