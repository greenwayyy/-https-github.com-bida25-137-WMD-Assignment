FEBRUARY 2026

IS IT ALL REAL OR DO WE MAKE IT REAL

Is being human the most selfish gift a mother can give their child? Or is giving one life without their consent selfish? I guess for these questions one would have to look at it in a philosophical point of view, human beings have inhabitat the earth for centuries and you would think that makes us special right? But no it does not, apparently our conscious and ability to reason is what makes us different and special from all these other species for some God to decide we were fit to rule this world? Let me guess "some God" threw you off but if you think deeply about it you get to realise it does not matter. I am writing a piece on how I feel about a lady giving birth to me without my consent and for some reason I dragged God into this because I have to blame someone, anyone. Confusing right? For me all of this makes sense in my head and I believe that I am right but you possibly dont which is okay. Because there are things which I find absolutely unfathomable which you think are right and simple. That is the beauty of being human you cannot tell me that there are things and ways a chicken does and believes are right which are completely different from another chicken, but us we get to have complex lives and still co-exit together.

JANUARY 2026

I'LL ONLY HAVE YOU IF YOU ARE SWEEETER THAN MY SOLITUDE

As someone who would definitely choose their own solitude over attending my mom or dad's funeral, I would whole heartedly prefer to be sitting in a comfy chair and with a gown on, sipping coffee and if I'm lucky that day the weather is gloomy. That's not really happening though now is it? For some reason (again I know the reason I'm just being ignorant) there are going to say "no you should go and get a chance to mourn them so you dont regret missing it" but I really believe it is not that DEEP. I mean I care about my parents and loved ones but just not enough for the situation to be a whole emotional rollercoaster. I only feel such emotions when it is about me but if not then I do not feel obligated to react. Then comes death I am sure by now you want to know what I think about it, well we cannot talk about death and not mention living you know? They co-exist, its kinda of romantic if you think about it long enough. Say, for you to die you should have atleast lived its sort of the requirement and you are probably asking "what about stillborn infants?" Well just cause you have'nt felt sunshine or seen moonlight does not mean you have not been alive it is sad but whatever.For me death is as simple as the concept of picking your nose, it's going to happen anyway so why fear it? And I think it is one of the simplest process our human bodies go through "to just shut down".

JANUARY 2026

THE GRIEF OF MOURNING SOMEONE WHO IS ALIVE

We never truly talk about the people we have cut off our lives, who once played an important role in our everyday choices even if it is "which ice-cream flavour should I get?" As that happened you were happy but it is that kind of happiness that you cannot measured because it does not happen for everyone like getting a new car or buying a new house that is a measurable achievement reduced to happiness. But its the kind of happiness that embraces your soul, you get fulfilled and then the lamp gets brighter everything changes, the person changes, they get a scholoarship move to a new city, begin college and you become happy for them because this is a next step in their life you have to support them even if it means you end up without them because it is not important. Now it all happened, your friends and family tell you that your life is still yet to be lived, more people to love, learn but we rarely talk about how our souls never really let us forget people who made us happy through these small unmeasurable moments. I think that we lose more people than we bury because everything is expected to keep going forward; even in mourning, we humans dont give ourselves enough time to get a break and allow a feeling sit or just nothing to sit in silence for our body to let it in and for our soul to embrace it. Instead we quickly talk about moving on to better things as if we are promised a better future, so our minds tend to grief the people we wished to have been there all along.

JANUARY 2026

Psychology Of Human Connection

Most of us have truly perfected the art of being normal that it just comes off effortlessly, you can see it in the way they interact in a social setting they know exactly what to say, the right body language, making sure they don't laugh too hard that the other member in the group feels uncomfortable. All this could it be empathy or performance? because everybody is so obsessed with being accepted by society that they do not want to feel left out. It is quite an uncomfortable gift to be human, one part of you yearns to be understood and the other part is tired of putting up this performance that makes you more palatable to society. Because when you are not being easy to be around the whole world is going to tell you "stop acting like that" "they wont like you if you do that" "do this instead" "wear that top instead you get more compliments in it anyway". Now it feels as though all we do is to impress the system because i cannot say it is to impress others because they too are performing, so who are we doing it for? A God? cause when we do things that only please ourselves then its wrong suddenly you are given the side eye like being cast out. Why is it that way though i could tell you about how the patriarchy set that system but we have heard many conversations about that around that its just not worth getting into. but what i can tell you is there is way you can break out of that performance and to do that you have to change your audience so instead of everybody you are going to please yourself, the voices in your head and that imaginary being you keep ignoring yes that one. You have to understand that humans take pleasure in being seen so to break free from that societal performance you would have to see yourself in such a fervent way that you would get shocked that other humans see you because with them its surface level but YOU, you have to see, feel and embrace your soul to the point where you believe you are God. Yes God, and once you start feeling that inner power your perception of the world changes everything you thought you knew and believed in becomes meaningless scary right? But fear not cause this means you get to create your own inner world where you decide what's important and how you react to the outside world you get to give them meaning to your world, feels superior right? What you need to realize is that the only one who should have control over your world is you, cause once you have mastered that then nothing can get to you unless you let it. A friend screws you over? Cool understand that you can only get hurt because that person has intentions to hurt you but you don't have the intention to get hurt so keep it that way no need to feel just cause somebody else told you how to feel

The Art Of Detachment

The idea of being completely consumed by what you desire mostly, knowing very well you could never submit yourself to it because it is going to end. But is it human to fear what you love most? There is no right answer to that question because some of us don't want to experience the pain of heartbreak, yes I want all the good that comes with love but the bad no thank you. I just don't know if I can deal with a version of me that's hurt and I don't want to find out. So I will carry on living life avoiding any chance of wearing my heart on my sleeve.

JANUARY 2026

Melancholy Is The Happiness Of Being Sad

There is a profound sadness my gut carries that I could never truly explain where it comes from and I am not even sure if I have always carried this sadness within me since childhood or did the feeling just make itself known as I grew older. To feel deeply truly is an uncomfortable gift and no I will not go around bragging that I am an empath just because for the moment I felt sad i decided to think about everything else that I cared about, be it people or that cat I saw idling all alone in the woods wondering if it feels like it needs a mother to lead it to warmth.Those rare days were I get those random doses of sadness is when I get to understand how not only how I feel but how my father whom I despise feels, I get to see him as a man who does not have a daughter to check up on him and ask if he is okay and how certain situations affected him and changed his perspective of life. When I am sad it is when I truly care but this is rare.
